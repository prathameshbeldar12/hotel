Welcome to Chalisgaon

Comfort, Convenience & Gracious Service

Located in the heart of Chalisgaon on Hirapur Road, near Bapji Hospital, **Hotel Annapurna Residence** (Hotel Annapura Residency) offers a peaceful refuge with contemporary facilities. Our goal is to provide a home away from home for travelers, featuring excellent lodging services and wedding event booking facilities in the Jalgaon district.

Whether visiting nearby holy sites like the Gajanan Maharaj Temple, relaxing at Narmada Park, or attending a celebration in our grand banquet hall, our attentive staff is dedicated to making your journey smooth and comfortable.
